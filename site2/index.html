About the Film

Ms.Marvel(TV series)

Ms. Marvel is an American television miniseries created by Bisha K. Ali for the streaming service Disney+, based on the Marvel Comics featuring the character Kamala Khan / Ms. Marvel. It is the seventh television series in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) produced by Marvel Studios, sharing continuity with the films of the franchise.

Cast and characters

A 16-year-old Pakistani-American high school student from Jersey City who is an aspiring artist and avid gamer, and writes superhero fan fiction about heroes such as Captain Marvel.Khan gains the ability to harness cosmic energy and create hard light constructs from a magical bangle.Executive producer Sana Amanat said Khan brought a "grounded perspective" to the MCU with "bright and eager eyes".
